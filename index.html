TERMS OF SERVICE
Last updated: March 2026 — CollabInst Platform

1. Platform Overview

CollabInst is a marketplace connecting Instagram creators with brands. We facilitate deals, content delivery and payments. CollabInst takes a 20% platform fee on every completed deal.

2. Content Ownership & Copyright

  • All content created by a creator remains their intellectual property until full payment is confirmed and rights are formally transferred.
  • Preview versions of reels are watermarked and are strictly for review purposes only.
  • Brands may not use, download, share or publish any watermarked preview content under any circumstances.
  • Using preview content without completing payment constitutes copyright infringement and will result in immediate account termination and legal action.
  • Content rights transfer to the brand only after full payment is confirmed by CollabInst admin.

3. Creator Obligations

  • Creators must deliver original, high quality content that matches the agreed deal description.
  • Creators must not upload inappropriate, sexual, violent or misleading content. Violations result in immediate permanent ban.
  • If the deal includes posting on the creator's Instagram account, the post must remain live for a minimum of 30 days.
  • Early deletion of a required Instagram post before the 30-day period will result in withholding of the second payment instalment.
  • Creators must provide proof of posting (Instagram post URL and screenshot) within 48 hours of the agreed posting date.
  • All uploaded content is reviewed by CollabInst admin before being shown to brands. This is non-negotiable.

4. Brand Obligations

  • Brands must clearly describe the content they need before a deal is initiated.
  • Brands must pay the full agreed amount to CollabInst before content creation begins.
  • Brands may not use preview/watermarked content for any purpose.
  • Brands must review and respond to delivered content within 7 days. Failure to respond will be treated as automatic approval.
  • Brands may request one free revision per deal. Additional revisions may incur extra charges agreed between brand and creator.
  • Attempting to steal, copy or use content without proper payment will result in permanent ban and legal action.

5. Payment Terms

  • All payments are made to CollabInst first. CollabInst holds funds in escrow until content is approved.
  • Creator receives 80% of the agreed deal amount. CollabInst retains 20% as platform fee.
  • For deals requiring Instagram posting, payment is split: 40% on reel delivery and approval, 40% after 30-day posting period is complete.
  • CollabInst reserves the right to withhold or refund payment in case of disputes, fraud or policy violations.
  • All payments are final once released. No refunds after content is approved and rights transferred.

6. Message Monitoring & Privacy

  • All messages sent on CollabInst are stored securely and may be reviewed by CollabInst admin for fraud detection, dispute resolution and platform safety purposes.
  • CollabInst monitors messages to detect platform bypass attempts — where brands and creators agree to conduct deals outside the platform to avoid platform fees.
  • Any attempt to move deals outside CollabInst will result in immediate permanent ban of both parties.
  • Messages are never shared with third parties and are only used for internal platform safety purposes.
  • By using CollabInst you consent to this message monitoring policy.

6. Content Moderation

  • All uploaded reels are reviewed by CollabInst admin before being shown to brands.
  • CollabInst reserves the right to reject any content that violates our community guidelines.
  • Rejected content will be returned to the creator with a reason. Creator may resubmit revised content.
  • Repeated violations will result in permanent account termination.

7. Dispute Resolution

  • In case of disputes between brands and creators, CollabInst admin has final authority.
  • Both parties must provide evidence to support their claims.
  • CollabInst will make a fair decision based on the deal terms, delivered content and platform policies.
  • CollabInst is not liable for disputes arising from unclear deal descriptions or undocumented agreements made outside the platform.

8. Account Termination

  • CollabInst reserves the right to terminate any account that violates these terms.
  • Terminated accounts forfeit any pending payments if termination is due to policy violations.
  • Users may delete their own accounts at any time from their profile settings.
